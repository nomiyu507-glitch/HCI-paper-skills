# Paper Types

Use this reference only when the paper type changes what should be emphasized.

## Survey / Review

Prioritize:

- taxonomy quality
- coverage boundaries
- framing of the field
- whether the categorization is genuinely useful
- missing subfields or biased coverage

Down-rank:

- low-level equation detail unless central to the taxonomy

## Method / Model Paper

Prioritize:

- exact problem definition
- novelty relative to the closest methods
- mechanism and objective design
- ablations and evidence quality

## System Paper

Prioritize:

- system assumptions
- architecture and workflow
- deployment constraints
- efficiency, scalability, and trade-offs
- realism of evaluation setup

## Theory Paper

Prioritize:

- theorem statement scope
- assumptions
- proof intuition
- what the theory actually explains or fails to explain

Avoid pretending formal rigor is stronger than it is if assumptions are narrow.

## Benchmark / Dataset Paper

Prioritize:

- construction process
- annotation quality
- representativeness
- leakage risks
- evaluation protocol
- what new comparisons the benchmark really enables

## Application Paper

Prioritize:

- domain relevance
- adaptation of prior methods
- practical constraints
- whether the contribution is methodological, empirical, or mostly integrative

## Design / HCI Paper

Prioritize:

- problem situatedness
- user/task context
- methodology choice
- contribution type
- design rationale
- limits of generalization

Be careful not to evaluate purely with benchmark-paper expectations.
